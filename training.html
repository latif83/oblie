Media Workshop

On Friday, 17th March 2023. University of Ghana SRC in partnership with OCC Media Network organized a Free Media & Secretariat Workshop for all students. I was honored to have been invited to handle the media aspect of it. I am absolutely grateful for where I am on my journey and the evident grace of God at work in me.
I want to thank UGSRC executives, especially Emmanuella Boakye Yiadom, the current UGSRC General Secretary, for trusting my brand with this huge task. Kudos to Narteh Joshua for handling the photography aspect of the training. The attendance and reactions after the training were heartwarming. Looking forward to getting more of such opportunities... #PossessingtheNations #makingimpact. Skill training outside the usual academic work helps students to gain confidence and become more flexible when faced with change. Getting such skills could prepare them for their careers and lives after school
